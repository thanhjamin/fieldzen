The Job

At Jova, we are developing a SaaS product that encompasses mobile and web platforms. As the Software Architect, you will play a key role in developing the architecture of our front-end system, applying their engineering techniques to complex, multi-tiered applications that directly touch the end-user experience and extend all the way to the back end components. You will take the lead in architecting and implementing the front end of platform utilizing HTML/CSS/JavaScript with popular open source libraries such as Backbone.js.

We are looking for a motivated individual with deep knowledge in full MVC Javascript stack and general web development experience. As we are at an early stage, you will also be exposed to various functions and be provided with many opportunities to grow the company.
