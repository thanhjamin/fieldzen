Web Architect at ResolutionTube - Jova

ResolutionTube’s new product Jova is on a mission to build a universal product-troubleshooting platform that empowers users by giving them access to the right information at right time. This includes instant access to domain experts on their mobile device.


The Company

Jova is a Seattle-based, early stage startup focused on building the first intelligent SAAS platform to reinvent the mobile workforce management. We’re a team of passionate, driven individuals who have built some amazing products at Amazon, Disney, MSFT, Hulu, Groupon, and are alums from IIT, DCE, CMU (Carnegie Mellon), University of Michigan. We’re accelerated through TechStars (2013 grad) and are now backed by Madrona Venture Group (early investors in Amazon, Redfin, Isilon and others).

At Jova, we challenge one another to improve with every project we take on and pride ourselves on our ability to create innovative products that delight our customers. We are looking for talented, bold and driven developers to join our stellar team who have a passion for building quality products that help reinvent an industry.

The Job

At Jova, we are developing a SaaS product that encompasses mobile and web platforms. As the Software Architect, you will play a key role in developing the architecture of our front-end system, applying their engineering techniques to complex, multi-tiered applications that directly touch the end-user experience and extend all the way to the back end components. You will take the lead in architecting and implementing the front end of platform utilizing HTML/CSS/JavaScript with popular open source libraries such as Backbone.js.

We are looking for a motivated individual with deep knowledge in full MVC Javascript stack and general web development experience. As we are at an early stage, you will also be exposed to various functions and be provided with many opportunities to grow the company.

Requirements

  • BS or MS in Computer Science or related technical field
  • Proficient in architecting and implementing web stack in accordance with RESTful backend services
  • 5+ years of experience in web development with proficiency in Javascript and CSS
  • 2+ years of experience in JavaScript programming in a MVC, REST API and Single Page Application environment (Angular or Backbone)
  • Proficient in two or more of JavaScript, Backbone, Angular, JQuery, HTML development
  • Experience with Agile Software Development techniques
  • Experience in consuming 3rd party APIs such as Parse, Google Analytics, Airbrake, etc.
  • Nice-to-have experiences:
    • Experience in Modernizer.js, Node.js and NPM
    • Experience in Git
    • Experience in leadership role

  • A highly competitive salary and substantial equity grants
