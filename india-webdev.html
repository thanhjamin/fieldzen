Web Developer at ResolutionTube - Jova (India)

ResolutionTube’s new product Jova is on a mission to build a universal product-troubleshooting platform that empowers users by giving them access to the right information at right time. This includes instant access to domain experts on their mobile device.


The Company

Jova is a Seattle-based, early stage startup focused on building the first intelligent SAAS platform to reinvent the mobile workforce management. We’re a team of passionate, driven individuals who have built some amazing products at Amazon, Disney, MSFT, Hulu, Groupon, and are alums from IIT, DCE, CMU (Carnegie Mellon), University of Michigan. We’re accelerated through TechStars (2013 grad) and are now backed by Madrona Venture Group (early investors in Amazon, Redfin, Isilon and others).

At Jova, we challenge one another to improve with every project we take on and pride ourselves on our ability to create innovative products that delight our customers. We are looking for talented, bold and driven developers to join our stellar team who have a passion for building quality products that help reinvent an industry.

The Job

At Jova, we are developing a SaaS product that encompasses mobile and web platforms. As the Web Developer, you will

  • Develop and maintain cutting-edge front-end websites and application components
  • Collaborate seamlessly with product development team through the entire development lifecycle to deliver rich, intuitive web user interfaces for optimizing the overall product user experience
  • Analyze requirements, contribute to software design specifications and suggest alternative solutions
  • Conduct tests to verify the functionality, accuracy and efficiency of software components; troubleshoot and resolve problems as necessary

Our team ships code at a rapid pace to meet customer requirements and pushes the boundaries of mobile and web development. As the member of the Jova team, you must be inspired by technology and will love working with a team of highly skilled people, whether they are in the office next to you or working remotely.

Requirements

  • BS or MS in Computer Science or related technical field
  • 3+ years of experience developing highly available web applications
  • Ability to create fast, efficient web applications with rich, innovative interfaces using the up-to-date client-side technologies HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery
  • Excellent working knowledge of JavaScript MVC frameworks preferably Node.js, Backbone.js
  • Good debugging and problem solving skills using Web Debuggers, HTTP Protocols, TCP/IP Networking and Operating Systems
  • Nice to have but not essential
    • Experience working with relational database systems (SQL) and NoSQL
    • Experience managing the offshore team deliverables is highly preferred
    • Familiarity with WebSockets, browser offline support is a plus

About You

  • You are empathetic and collaborative with teammates
  • You are ambitious and hungry to make an impact
  • You are committed to building a flawless customer experience
  • You are comfortable with working at a fast pace and in an open environment

What You'll Gain

  • Ground floor opportunity within the India team; chance to shape the strategic direction of the company
  • A chance to work on challenging problems and exercise creativity and leadership in your work
  • US onsite experience: Travel to and from Seattle to build rapport with core engineering team members located there
  • Work with sharp, ambitious and challenging co-workers in a fun, collaborative environment
  • Opportunity to experiment with cutting edge technology such as Google Glass and augmented reality
  • A rare opportunity to build products for a prominent, relevant, and real-life problem and change the course of an entire industry
  • A highly competitive salary; desired work set-up; company sponsored events and happy hours

If solving a real pain point for millions of people gets you excited, we'd love to speak with you.
